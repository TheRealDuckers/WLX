About Us

WLX Productions strives to provide the highest standards in all aspects of AV, lighting, sound and staging, as well as theatrical servicing, installation, inspection and testing. We take pride in providing quality solutions efficiently, professionally and with the attention to detail that our clients expect. From dry hire to full technical event management, we can help. We have a wealth of experience in providing complete turnkey packages for our clients. Our professionals undertake the design, supply and realisation of your project. For concert halls, theatres, conferences, schools and parties, we can bring you the custom installation you require to meet your needs.
